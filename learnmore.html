What is Pancreatitis?

Pancreatitis is inflammation of the pancreas. Chronic pancreatitis is long term inflammation which does not improve or gets worse over time and may lead to permanent damage and various health problems. Long term damage may lead to scarring of the pancreas, insufficient production of enzymes necessary for food digestion, poor production of insulin which can lead to diabetes, and other major health concerns.

Signs and Symptoms of Pancreatitis

  • Upper abdominal pain that radiates into the back
    • Sometimes aggravated by eating - especially foods high in fat
  • Swollen and tender abdomen
  • Nausea ad vomiting
  • Fever
  • Increased heart rate
  • Weight loss due to poor nutrient absorption.
